A Wish For Power

A world where you have a chance to get your dreams of strength and vengeance, as long as the Deity smiles upon you. Half the people it grants with ability go mad, and if you don't? You'll survive by killing them.

Our main character is a man who calls himself 'S', or Ess, a fire wielder who acts the opposite. Cold and efficient to just about everyone, but if you manage to piss him off, someone's going to turn up with life-altering burns or as DNA-tested ash.
The story follows him and his squad of Gifts as they hunt down rouge Gifts and deal with government control. It sounds dumb in a summary.

Squad 1-S consists of Ess,
Maven, Ess's lover, a bubbly and cheerful enby
Eli, smart and good with his hands,
Buff, not that smart but has a Gift for strength,
And a girl with a demonic lie detector in an eye socket.
