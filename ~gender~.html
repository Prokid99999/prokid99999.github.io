List of genders I've used

Keep in mind this is not how all people feel; these are only my understanding of genders I have used for my characters.

Cis Male & Cis Female: Self-explainitory. You identify as a man/woman. (he/him and she/her)

Nonbinary[enby]: You identify as something other than male or female. Nonbinary/enby is a blanket term for anybody who doesn't identify as cis male or female, so it covers these genders:
Demigirl: You identify as female sometimes but not always. (she/they, they/her, something like that)
Demiboy: You identify as male sometimes but not always. (he/they, they/him, something like that)
Agender: You are genderless. (they/them)
