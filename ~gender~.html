List of genders I've used

Keep in mind this is not how all people feel; these are only my understanding of genders I have used for my characters.

Cis Male & Cis Female: Self-explainitory. Identifies as a man/woman. (he/him and she/her)

Nonbinary[enby]: Identifies as something other than male or female. Nonbinary/enby is a blanket term for anybody who doesn't identify as cis male or female[im pretty sure], so it covers these genders:
Genderfluid: Shifts between multiple genders, identifying as one sometimes and another at other times. (ask for pronouns or use they/them)
Demigirl: Identifies as female sometimes but not always. (she/they, they/her, something like that)
Demiboy: Identifies as male sometimes but not always. (he/they, they/him, something like that)
Agender: Genderless; doesn't identify as any gender. (they/them)
