Cursed Technique: Virtual Idol
1st Grade Sorcerer

Miku Hatsune made a Binding Vow sacrificing her ability to generate her own cursed energy for the ability to siphon cursed energy from her fans.
Her cursed technique consists of a beam of cursed energy called "Miku Miku Beam." It is activated by saying "beam" and holding two fingers pointed in a 'V' next to an eye. "Miku" can be said up to twice before "beam" as a sort of chant, and another hand can be used to increase power further.

The full chant is:
"And now..."
"It's time!"
"For the moment you've been waiting for!"
"One!"
"Two!"
"Three!"
"Ready?"
"Miku Miku BEAM!"

Domain Expansion: Colorful Stage
The inside of this domain is, as the name implies, a stage with a crowd. Hatsune is placed onstage as a performer while others are placed in the crowd.
Its purpose is to give Hatsune time to say the full chant of Miku Miku Beam.
Hatsune uses a full-body pose to activate her domain, as opposed to the traditional hand sign. The only requirement is "a pose an idol would strike onstage."
