Cursed Technique: Virtual IdolTETO
Grade 4 Sorcerer

Her cursed technique allows her to take on attributes of variations on Kasane Teto. For example, she could use Tetoris Teto and become "the girl who arranges the blocks from up above", causing Tetris blocks to appear in the sky and slam down onto her target.

The danger of this technique comes wih both the sheer number of personas at Kasane's disposal. Her technique isn't nearly as refined as Hatsune's, but with training she could become at least Grade 1.


Domain Expansion: Teto's Territory

Kasane uses a full-body pose to activate her domain, as opposed to the usual hand sign. The requirement is "a pose an idol would strike onstage."

Within this domain, Kasane chooses the interpretation of each person inside. She can make her enemies weaker and bring her allies to their strongest.
