Cursed Technique: Virtual IdolMIKU
Special Grade Sorcerer

Miku Hatsune made a Binding Vow sacrificing her ability to generate her own cursed energy for the ability to siphon cursed energy from her fans. This vow manifested in Hatsune's abiliy to portray emotion; or, rather, in her lack of it. Her eyes are always blank no matter what expression she puts on her face, her voice has a distinct lack of variation when using her normal tone, and while she can change her tone and pitch, it requires much effort to mimic emotion.

One benefit of this vow is that because Hatsune cannot generate cursed energy, she can expend unnecessary cursed energy to leave herself with none. See 'Fushiguro Toji' and 'Zen'in Maki' for more information about this condition.

Another is that if her opponent falls under its conditions, she can deprive them of their cursed energy and end the fight before it starts.

Her cursed technique consists of one ability: taking on attributes of variations on Hatsune Miku. For example, she can don the nurse's outfit of M@GICAL CURE! LOVE SHOT! Miku and gain the ability to transfer a person's injuries to herself. This persona also grants an offensive technique called 'Miku Miku Beam', a laser of cursed energy aimed from the eyes.

The danger of this technique comes with both the sheer number of personas and the raw volume of cursed energy at Hatsune's disposal. She has no reasonable limit on her output and incredibly precise control over it, making her power difficult to gauge. She could ruffle your hair or flatten Japan. Maybe she could split the earth. Only she knows.

Domain Expansion: Colorful Stage

Hatsune uses a full-body pose to activate her domain, as opposed to the usual hand sign. The requirement is "a pose an idol would strike onstage."

Within this domain, every interpretation of the people inside take form.

She looks 16 and travels with Luka because she got tired of people treating her as a child, as well as the fact that people are uncomfortable with her alone.
