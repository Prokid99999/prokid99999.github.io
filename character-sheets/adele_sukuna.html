Adele Sukuna is an orphaned relative of Ryomen Sukuna. They inherited the Shrine technique, though when they were brought to Jujutsu Tech they could barely put a mark on a tree. Jujutsu society's higher-ups deemed them a non-threat and they were allowed to live after not showing progress with their technique for many months, then multiple years.

Through unknown methods, Adele was transported to Terraria and completed both the main boss progression and the contents of the Calamity Mod. They used these months to hone their cursed technique, physical ability and combat experience to that of a Special Grade.
